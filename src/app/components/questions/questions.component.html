How to buy Big Blue Whale Token ?
You can buy BBWT on PancakeSwap. To do that, you need to have Metamask extansion. If you have metamask extansion on your browser and an Binance Smart Chain user which has an Account with balance, in the swap section of PancakeSwap, you can buy our tokens. In the swap section, when you select the token you want to have, you need to paste our contract address, and then our token will appear and you will be able to swap.
How many tokens are deployed in Pancakeswap ?
As it says in Distribution info, 70% of all token supply which is equal 70,000,000 tokens are deployed to PancakeSwap. The other 30,000,000 tokens are held in development team's account. These 30,000,000 tokens will be sold time by time in several years as small amounts (%1>) to create funding to develop new projects for BWPT.
Is liquidity on PancakeSwap locked ?
Yes, the liquidity on PancakeSwap is locked which means there can't be no rug pull. When you invest to our token and buy some, there is no way that its value can be zero.
